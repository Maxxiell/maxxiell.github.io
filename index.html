Purpose In Motion

I'm Maciel — Driven by curiosity, tinkering, tech and a lifelong connections to arts. Since a young age, I immersed myself in all shapes and forms of it. Spending hours sketching, painting, breaking things down and putting them back together. That early love for hands on creativity and inquisitiveness to know more became the foundation for everything I do — evolving into music, photo, design, cars, software and various other outlets. In my search for resonance, clarity, emotion and logical thinking; these experiences guide my work into meaningful and thoughtful decisions. I move fluidly between these disciplines with the goal of consistency.

"I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious."
— Albert Einstein

That curiosity — not just about how things work, but why they feel the way they do — is what drives everything I build.
