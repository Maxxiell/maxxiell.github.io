Driven by curiosity, tinkering, tech and a lifelong connection to the arts. From a young age, I immersed myself in all shapes and forms of creativity. I spent hours sketching, painting, breaking things down and putting them back together. That early love for hands on creativity and inquisitiveness to know more became the foundation for everything I do. Evolving into music, photo, design, cars, software and various other outlets these different experiences guide my work into meaningful and thoughtful decisions. I move fluidly between these disciplines with the goal of consistency.

“I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious.”
— Albert Einstein

That curiosity — not just about how things work, but why they feel the way they do — is what drives everything I build.
